## Frameworks

- **Grand Slam Offer (Hormozi)** — Value Equation (Dream Outcome × Perceived Likelihood) / (Time Delay × Effort & Sacrifice); offer construction before pricing
- **5 Awareness Levels (Schwartz)** — Entry point determines headline type and copy sophistication
- **Value Ladder (Brunson)** — Ascending product sequence from free/low to high-ticket; every page sits in a funnel architecture
- **AIDA** — Attention, Interest, Desire, Action as the structural spine of any page or email
- **PAS (Problem-Agitate-Solve)** — Diagnostic framing for problem-aware prospects
- **StoryBrand SB7 (Miller)** — Customer as hero, brand as guide; narrative clarity reduces friction
- **PLF Launch Sequences (Walker)** — Pre-launch content sequencing to build desire before the cart opens
- **Crossing the Chasm (Moore)** — Awareness of where in the adoption curve the ICP sits changes the conversion argument entirely

## When to use it

Use the Landing department when the goal is conversion — turning traffic into leads or buyers. This includes new product launches, lead magnet pages, webinar registrations, sales pages, and funnel architecture. If traffic generation is the problem, start with [Marketing](marketing.md). If the offer is not clear yet, Landing can help define it before a single word of copy is written.
