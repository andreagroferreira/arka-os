The Marketing & Growth department drives sustainable acquisition, retention, and revenue across every channel. Luna's squad operates at the intersection of data and psychology — diagnosing where growth is leaking, designing self-reinforcing loops, and running the paid, organic, and content engines in parallel.

Every output is framework-backed. Growth decisions reference the AARRR funnel and Growth Loop canon. Content decisions are grounded in Eugene Schwartz awareness stages — matching message sophistication to where the prospect actually stands, not where you wish they were.

## Frameworks

- **AARRR Pirate Metrics (McClure)** — Acquisition, Activation, Retention, Revenue, Referral as the diagnostic scaffold for every growth audit
- **Growth Loops (Chen / Reforge)** — Self-reinforcing cycles that compound; replaces linear funnel thinking
- **5 Awareness Levels (Schwartz)** — Unaware through Most Aware; message matched to prospect sophistication
- **PLG Flywheel (Bush)** — Product as the primary acquisition and expansion driver
- **STEPPS Viral Framework (Berger)** — Social Currency, Triggers, Emotion, Public, Practical Value, Stories
- **Content Pillar-Cluster SEO** — Topical authority built through hierarchical content architecture
- **E-E-A-T + Core Web Vitals** — Technical and trust signals for organic search performance

## What you can ask for

- Run a full SEO audit covering technical issues, on-page gaps, and link profile: `/mkt seo-audit`
- Design a growth loop that compounds — viral, paid, content, or product: `/mkt growth-loop`
- Build a 90-day content calendar mapped to awareness levels and keywords: `/mkt calendar-plan`
- Audit the current content library for gaps, cannibalization, and update priority: `/mkt content-audit`
- Launch a paid campaign across Meta, Google, or TikTok with creative brief and budget allocation: `/mkt paid-campaign`
- Write a cold email sequence for outbound prospecting: `/mkt cold-email`
- Set up marketing automation flows for lead nurture and lifecycle triggers: `/mkt marketing-automation`
- Segment the audience by behavior and readiness: `/mkt audience-segment`
- Run a competitor analysis across positioning, channels, and messaging: `/mkt competitor-analysis`
- Design and analyze an A/B test for any marketing asset: `/mkt ab-test`

## When to use it

Bring in Marketing when you need to grow — whether that is diagnosing why a funnel is leaking, building the channels that feed it, or systematizing a repeatable acquisition engine. The department also owns SEO, paid media, email, and social strategy. For offer creation or landing page copy, hand off to [Landing Pages](landing.md). For distribution and content production at volume, involve [Content](content.md).
