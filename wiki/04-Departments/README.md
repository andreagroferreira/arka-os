with a command prefix; the seventeenth, the Quality Gate, is cross-cutting and
runs automatically on every workflow.

(Tier 2/3). Click through for the full roster, frameworks, and commands.

## The C-Suite spans departments

Six Tier-0 agents hold veto power across all departments: **Marco** (CTO),
**Helena** (CFO, also leads Finance), **Sofia** (COO, also leads Organization),
**Marta** (CQO, leads Quality), **Eduardo** (Copy Director), and **Francisca**
(Tech & UX Director). Eduardo and Francisca are the two reviewers Marta
dispatches on the [Quality Gate](../10-Quality-Gate.md).

## How to use a department

You rarely call a department directly. Describe what you need and the `/do`
orchestrator routes it (see [Commands Reference](../05-Commands-Reference.md)).
When you want to be explicit, use the prefix — `/dev`, `/mkt`, `/brand`, and so
on.
