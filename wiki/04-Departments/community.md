# Communities

← [Departments index](README.md) · [Home](../Home.md)

> **Prefix:** `/community` · **Lead:** Beatriz (Tier 1) · **Agents:** 3 · **Skills:** 14

The Communities department designs, launches, and operates member communities across platforms — Discord, Telegram, Skool, Circle, and others. Beatriz approaches every community as a business model first: purpose, ideal member profile, value proposition, and monetization must be defined before any platform is chosen or content is produced. The squad covers the full lifecycle from niche selection and platform setup through onboarding, engagement mechanics, moderation, and retention.

Tania handles gamification and engagement loops — points, levels, challenges, streaks. Maria runs daily community management, member activation, and the content calendar. Together the squad is built to grow paid communities that retain members beyond the initial excitement spike.

## Frameworks

- **SPACES Framework (Spinks)** — Support, Product, Acquisition, Contribution, Engagement, Success; defines what role the community plays in the business
- **Community Business Model Canvas** — maps purpose, member profile, value proposition, content engine, monetization, and platform in one view
- **Member Lifecycle (Aware → Advocate)** — five-stage journey used to design onboarding, activation, and retention interventions at each phase
- **1000 True Fans (Kevin Kelly)** — sets the strategic north star: depth of relationship matters more than raw member count
- **Membership Economy (Baxter)** — recurring value through subscription, not one-off transactions; drives monetization model design
- **TRIBE Method (McLaren)** — identity-based community building; members join for belonging, not just content
- **Gamification mechanics** — points, leaderboard, badges, streaks, and challenge design applied through Tania's engagement loop work

## When to use it

Use the Communities department when you are launching a new community from scratch, trying to reactivate a stalled one, designing a monetization model, or solving a retention problem. It is also the right department for platform migration decisions, gamification design, and event planning within an existing community.
