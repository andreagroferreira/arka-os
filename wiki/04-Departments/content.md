# Content & Viralization

← [Departments index](README.md) · [Home](../Home.md)

> **Prefix:** `/content` · **Lead:** Rafael (Tier 1) · **Agents:** 4 · **Skills:** 14

The Content & Viralization department is responsible for creating content that spreads. Rafael's squad works the full production chain — from hook design and script structure through to platform-native distribution and systematic repurposing. The output is not content for its own sake; it is content engineered to capture attention, hold it, and move it toward a business outcome.

The squad operates with a platform-first discipline: the same idea gets packaged differently for YouTube, Reels, TikTok, LinkedIn, and email. One pillar piece becomes ten atomic assets through a structured repurposing system.

## Frameworks

- **STEPPS Viral Framework (Berger)** — Social Currency, Triggers, Emotion, Public, Practical Value, Stories; the diagnostic for why content spreads
- **Hook Architecture (3-second rule)** — Seven hook types mapped to audience state; the first three seconds determine whether the rest gets watched
- **Content Operating System (GaryVee / Welsh)** — Pillar content atomized into platform-native micro-assets at scale
- **Pillar → Atomize Repurposing** — One long-form piece yields multiple short-form, newsletter, and social assets without duplication
- **Platform Algorithm Mechanics** — Native format and pacing constraints per platform (YouTube, Instagram Reels, TikTok, LinkedIn, X)
- **Content-to-Revenue Pipeline** — Mapping content assets to funnel stages and measuring attribution, not just reach

## What you can ask for

- Write hooks for the first three seconds of any video — seven types with A/B variants: `/content hook-write`
- Structure a complete video script (hook-bridge-body-CTA) for YouTube or Reels: `/content script-structure`
- Design a viral content piece using STEPPS mechanics: `/content viral-design`
- Build a 30 or 90-day content calendar across platforms: `/content calendar`
- Repurpose one piece of long-form content into a full multi-platform asset set: `/content repurpose-plan`
- Write a newsletter edition (story-driven, not broadcast): `/content newsletter-write`
- Create short-form content for Reels, TikTok, or YouTube Shorts: `/content short-form`
- Design a thumbnail and title package that increases click-through rate: `/content thumbnail-package`
- Build a YouTube channel strategy from positioning to upload cadence: `/content youtube-strategy`
- Set up a content system — production workflow, templates, publishing cadence: `/content content-system`

## When to use it

Bring in Content when you need volume, consistency, and distribution — not just a single asset. The department is most effective when there is already a clear audience, a defined voice, and a funnel to send traffic into. For offer and conversion copy, defer to [Landing Pages](landing.md). For SEO and channel strategy, coordinate with [Marketing](marketing.md). Content works best downstream of a clear positioning decision from [Brand](brand.md) or [Strategy](../04-Departments/README.md).
