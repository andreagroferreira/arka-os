# ArkaOS Wiki

**The Operating System for AI Agent Teams.**

> 82 agents · 17 departments · 267 skills · 4,500+ tests · multi-runtime.
> Every number on this wiki is generated by `scripts/tools/docs_stats.py` and
> locked by a test — it cannot drift from the code.

ArkaOS turns a single AI coding tool into an entire company. Instead of one
generalist assistant, you get specialized departments — development, brand,
finance, strategy, marketing, and more — each with a lead agent who
orchestrates specialists, all governed by a constitution and a mandatory
Quality Gate.

```bash
npx arkaos install
```

## How ArkaOS works in one diagram

```
You describe what you need (plain language)
        |
        v
Synapse (12-layer context injection) routes to the right department
        |
        v
Squad: lead agent orchestrates specialists with enterprise frameworks
        |
        v
Quality Gate (Marta + Eduardo + Francisca) reviews — APPROVED or REJECTED
        |
        v
Output: tested, reviewed, documented — saved to your Obsidian vault
        |
        v
Cognitive Layer captures the work; tomorrow ArkaOS is smarter than today
```
