# Community Business Model — `/community model <niche>`

> **Agent:** Beatriz (Community Strategist) | **Framework:** Community BMC + SPACES

## Community Business Model Canvas

| Block | Question |
|-------|----------|
| **Purpose** | Why does this community exist? What transformation does it offer? |
| **Ideal Member** | Who is the perfect member? Demographics, psychographics, goals |
| **Value Proposition** | What do members get that they can't get alone? (SPACES) |
| **Engagement Model** | How do members interact? Events, content, discussion, challenges |
| **Monetization** | How does it make money? (7 streams below) |
| **Retention** | What keeps members coming back month after month? |
| **Platform** | Where does it live? (Telegram, Discord, Skool, Circle, etc.) |
| **Growth** | How do new members discover and join? |

## SPACES Value Framework (David Spinks)

| Value | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **S**upport | Members help each other solve problems |
| **P**roduct | Community feedback improves the product |
| **A**cquisition | Community drives new customer acquisition |
| **C**ontribution | Members create content and value |
| **E**ngagement | Deeper engagement with brand/product |
| **S**uccess | Members achieve their goals faster together |

## 7 Revenue Streams

1. Monthly membership fee
2. Courses and educational content
3. 1-on-1 or group coaching
4. Live events (virtual and in-person)
5. Affiliate commissions
6. Sponsorships
7. Digital products (templates, tools)

## 1000 True Fans Math (Kevin Kelly)
- 1000 fans x $100/year = $100K/year
- 500 fans x $200/year = $100K/year
- 200 fans x $500/year = $100K/year
- Target: what's YOUR math?
